Wordsearch Generator

Instructions

The generator allows you to create a wordsearch puzzle with custom words. To begin, add a word in the box below. Press enter or click "add" after each word, you will see the words which have been added succesfully beneath the input box. When you are finished adding words, click "generate" to begin solving your custom word search puzzle!

Rules

  • Words must be between 4 - 10 letters
  • Words cannot be repeated
  • Letters A-Z only, no numbers or special characters
  • Anything mispelled at input will be mispelled in the puzzle
